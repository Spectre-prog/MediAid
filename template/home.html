About Us

Bridging the Healthcare Gap in Nigeria

Founded in 2022, MediAid NG was born out of a desire to make quality healthcare accessible to all Nigerians, regardless of location or economic status.

Our team combines medical professionals, AI experts, and local community health workers to create solutions that truly address Nigeria's unique healthcare challenges.

We partner with hospitals, pharmacies, and government health agencies to ensure our recommendations are accurate, up-to-date, and actionable.
